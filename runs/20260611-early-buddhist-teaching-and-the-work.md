## Findings Summary

The Work has partial early-discourse support when treated as a limited reflective inquiry into stressful views, reactive thought, and selfing. Strong parallels were MN 2, MN 18, MN 19, SN 36.6, DN 1, and AN 5.161-162; ethical checking and speech/action boundaries were anchored in MN 61, MN 58, and AN 3.66.

The unsupported or contrary parts were treating all suffering as belief in thoughts, replacing external truth and harm assessment with inward turnarounds, bypassing right speech/right action, and treating private inquiry as a sufficient stand-alone path outside right view and the gradual training.
