## What surprised me
- The Milindapañha is the single most important text for paritta's development — it names all seven parittas and provides the first systematic theory of efficacy. I expected commentary to be the bridge but the Milindapañha does more work.
- The Vinaya's explicit mention of paritta (guttatthāya parittaṃ) at vin02m1_mul 1016/1020 is earlier and more explicit than I expected. The canonical regulation of protective chanting is right there.
