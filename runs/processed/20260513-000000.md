## What surprised me
- The Vibhaṅga-aṭṭhakathā passage stating *ahosi-kammavipāka* on arahantship surfaced directly via a canon search for "diṭṭhadhammavedanīyaṃ" in `*_att` — a much more targeted hit than expected.
- Indavamsa's argument is internally coherent enough that the critique had to be structural (he reintroduces ahosi-kamma in different terminology) rather than dismissive.
- Calibre helper returned zero hits for "Amerikave", "Indavamsa", "17240", and "cessation of kamma" despite the book being present at the exact path. The helper's metadata index appears stale relative to the live library.

## What I'd change in the skill
- Document the Calibre metadata-staleness limitation more visibly. If FTS isn't ready AND metadata is stale, agents will silently miss user-owned books. Worth noting in the helper to refresh metadata.db before searching, or to log when the local library's mtime is newer than the helper's last index.
- Add a tip about commentary search via `--books "*_att"` for doctrinal questions — this is a much faster path to authoritative commentarial positions than going through Visuddhimagga / Vism-mahāṭīkā by hand.
