## What surprised me
- The "atthi" suttas (Ud 8.1, 8.3) are surprisingly explicit in their positive ontological assertions, providing strong support for the "reality" view.
- The distinction between *paramattha* (ultimate reality) in Abhidhamma and the "middle way" in the suttas is a key fault line in the debate.
