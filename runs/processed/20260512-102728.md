## What surprised me
- The Paṭisambhidhāmagga is not in the canon db searchable as a standalone text — I had to rely on commentary citations for its content. That's a gap in the helper's coverage.
- AN 8.63 and AN 4.123-126 explicitly link mettā to jhāna in the suttas — my initial draft had this wrong (claimed no jhāna link in suttas). The cross-check caught it.
- The Karaṇīyamettā Sutta (Khp 9) does contain wish-formulations — contrary to the common claim that "the suttas never use phrases."
- The vault already had a significant mettā/brahmavihāra analysis in the meditation-subjects note that I could build on.
