## What surprised me
- The Paṭisambhidāmagga (pre-commentary late Khuddaka) already defines pīti's aspect as "pharaṇaṭṭha" (pervading) — an undifferentiated bodily/mental proto-form that Visuddhimagga elaborates
- The Dhammasaṅgaṇī definition of pīti is entirely mental synonyms with no bodily component — the strongest canonical counter to the "bodily only" claim
- Sayadaw U Pandita's "only last two = real pīti" is a distinctive Burmese position not found in Dhammasaṅgaṇī or sutta layer
