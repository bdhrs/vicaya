## What surprised me
- MA162 (BDK) adds a lamp simile for parinibbāna entirely absent from MN140 — a substantive doctrinal addition, not just stylistic variation.
- The Vibhaṅga Chapter 3 preserves both Suttanta and Abhidhamma analyses of the six elements in adjacent sub-sections, exactly as it does for jhāna in Chapter 12 — a clean window into the Abhidhamma redactional process.
- SN35.245 (Kiṃsukopama) maps city-lord/consciousness onto MN140's framework explicitly, clarifying why viññāṇa is treated as the sixth element rather than relinquished with the other five.
- Pukkusāti is classified by the Papañcasūdanī as vipañcitaññū — this explains the extended discourse structure and is a key commentarial contribution not derivable from MN140 itself.
